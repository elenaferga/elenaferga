About me

I am a second-year PhD candidate at the Instituto de Astrofísica de Andalucía (IAA-CSIC), where I focus on understanding the fundamental nature of our universe through various cosmological approaches. My research combines theoretical work on cosmic voids, conformal models and numerical simulations.


As a member of the DESI collaboration, I contribute to advancing our understanding of dark energy through extensive simulation work. My expertise lies in applying the SHAM method to the Uchuu simulation, helping bridge the gap between theoretical models and observational data.
